After a second taste-profile synthesis against the **full 155-narrative corpus** (previously done against a 16-post subset), these structural findings matter for classification, content design, and memory of Loc as a person. Source: `/Users/deathemperor/death/pensieve/data/taste-profile.md` synthesized 2026-04-11.

1. **Fish-keeping is Loc's single largest theme — by far.** 45+ posts in the corpus. 2020 was almost entirely cichlid-breeding content (32 of 36 posts that year). Loc is a **species-level hobbyist**, co-admin of a Vietnamese "Rô Mỹ" (Central American cichlids) Facebook group, claims Vietnam-first breedings of Madagascar species. Earlier classifications that treated fish posts as "nature framed through family memory" were undercounting this — pure hobby/breeding logs exist and should land in `nature`, not `personal-stories`, when there's no family framing.

2. **Origin-story spine.** Post `08c80ce1` is Loc's explicit retelling of learning his parents considered not keeping the pregnancy, and "Lộc" (which means "blessing" in Vietnamese) is framed as a thank-you name. **This existential frame runs under many later reflective posts** that would otherwise read as generic philosophy. Family-gratitude-as-origin is a live motif, not a passing observation.

3. **Mental-health reframing.** Post `df3e6c63` documents Loc's 2018 bipolar diagnosis, which he reframes as "hai mùa mưa nắng" (two seasons, rain and sun) with 5–6 month "hố đen / mặt trời" (black hole / sun) cycles. A sư phụ (mentor/teacher) instructs him to **write when stuck**. This means many posts that look like "philosophy" are actually **confessional mental-health personal-stories** — classify them accordingly.

4. **The repost habit.** 11 near-duplicate clusters (24 posts total) in the corpus. Loc periodically reposts older writing, sometimes with small edits. The ingest script's dedupe handles exact duplicates; the classifier needs to treat near-duplicates as separate-but-linked entries and not burn effort over-classifying variants.

5. **2026 is a memoir sprint.** The 2026 posts are a dense autobiographical arc where Loc retells his whole career (VNG → Meta.us/Carina → Papaya → AI pivot), heavily name-tagging old friends (Huỳnh Gia Tài, Trần Hoàng Phong, Nguyễn Công Huân, Phương Bùi Minh). This is likely **the proximate reason pensieve exists** — Loc is actively working on a memoir-in-progress and wants the writing rescued from Facebook scroll.

**How to apply:**
- When classifying posts, these five priors override generic defaults. Fish posts = nature unless explicitly family-framed. "Reflective" posts = check for mental-health vocabulary before assigning philosophy. Named-friend posts = personal-stories even if topic is career.
- When choosing visual treatments or copy for the blog (taglines, landing page), the "memoir-in-progress" framing is true to Loc — lean into it rather than pretending pensieve is a generic tech blog.
- When writing about Loc in future sessions (empathy / context), these notes are load-bearing. He's not just "a senior engineer" — he's a writer rescuing his own history, with family-gratitude and mental-health as named structural frames.
