Click → Navigate Transition

What happens when you click a panel to enter a sub-page?

A

Panel Expand

The clicked panel scales up to fill the viewport while other panels fade out. Then the sub-page content fades in inside it. Like the door growing to full size before you step through.

click me

Uses View Transitions API (native in Chrome/Edge, progressive enhancement). Falls back to instant navigation in other browsers.

B

Flash + Dissolve

A brief accent-colored flash ripples from the clicked panel outward, then the entire page dissolves into the sub-page. Like a spell being cast — flash of light, new reality appears.

click me

Tiny JS for the ripple (click handler), CSS for the animation. View Transitions API for the page dissolve.

C

Morph Transition

The panel's title and accent color morph into the sub-page header using view-transition-name. The panel title flies up to become the page heading. Border color carries over. Everything else cross-fades. The most "seamless" feel — like the room reshaping around you.
