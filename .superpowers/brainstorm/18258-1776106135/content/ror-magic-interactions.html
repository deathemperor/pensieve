Magical Reveal Patterns

How should panels feel when they appear and open? Pick the vibe.

A

Staggered Materialize

Panels fade in one by one on page load with a slight upward drift — like objects appearing in the Room of Requirement. Each panel delayed 80ms after the previous. Pure CSS @keyframes + animation-delay.

Zero JS. CSS-only. Subtle and fast.

B

Shimmer Border on Hover

Navigable panels get a magical shimmer along their border on hover — a gradient that sweeps around the edge like a wand trace. Combined with a subtle glow. CSS background: conic-gradient on a pseudo-element.

Both: Materialize In + Shimmer Hover

Combine A and B. Panels materialize on load (staggered fade-up). Navigable panels shimmer on hover. The page feels like walking into the Room — things appear, then respond to your presence.

Full magical experience. Still CSS-only. No JS needed.
